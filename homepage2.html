background


Dedicated and seasoned Community Outreach Specialist with a superior record of successful community service and engagement. Adept at working with a wide variety of community demographics to formulate beneficial programs and issue resolutions. A result driven, self-motivated and resourceful project manager with a proven ability to develop and strengthen management teams to maximize company profit and reach goals. Possessing excellent communication skills and able to establish sustainable relationships with partnering organizations and corporations.

Veniece Newton, is a true entrepreneur of today who has been connecting people and products through her relationship building for more than a decade. In the early years, she gained her experience while working in promotions and marketing in the music industry. After graduating as a cum laude recipient from Fashion Institute of Technology and holding a Bachelors in Marketing from Indiana University of PA she then transitioned her love for music into fashion by becoming a Buyers Assistant at the world-renowned Barneys NYC and later as a stylist for recording artists, magazines and catalogs. These early years of learning her interests, allowed her to eventually find her way to what she truly loved , being involved in the community.

Over the years, she has developed an entrepreneurial spirit that could be denied. Her innovative and unparalleled ability to harness the power of relationship building has given her tremendous respect in the business community as a whole. With 15 years of experience in marketing she oversaw the television production of segments from Hong Kong, Alaska, Los Angeles, Miami, New York City and many other locations. By travelling the world, she was able to interview celebrities, cover red carpets for many high profile events and also gain additional experience from retail management, fashion styling, fashion buying, consulting, producing, project development and writing.

As a Philadelphia native, Veniece eventually became the Creative Director for a collection of theatrical show merchandise for the legendary Teddy Pendergrass play which debuted in Chicago in 2008. She developed the skills necessary to offer a fashion medium which led her to become a global traveler and consult in many other business ventures. Her writing and producing talents eventually led her to approach film and reality tv projects. To date, she has written and performed voiceovers for radio commercials for promotional events held in Philadelphia, written 3 movies scripts which are currently being presented to various production houses and have gotten a positive and tremendous response from the reality tv concept.

Eager to share her talents and resources with others, she has collaborated with various nonprofit and for-profit organizations to strengthen the awareness of sustainability. As a natural connector, she is a member of several committees and enjoys consulting others with their business ventures. She also acts as the Diversity Liaison for the Philadelphia International Airport in which she works specifically to improve workforce and developments and works alongside minority businesses. However, one of her other passions lies in working with youth. She not only has a flair for business and connecting people, but she also has a heart of gold. For the past 10 years, she has devoted her time to empowering the next generation of leaders, as the Chairwoman/ Founder of Community Angel Foundation; a nonprofit organization that gives back to the world by encouraging, empowering and educating our youth to be future leaders through 4 pillars focusing on volunteerism, education, career and culture. Today, the organization has several programs which enhances the lives of many through education, support and guidance.
